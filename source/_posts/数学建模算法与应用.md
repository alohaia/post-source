# 线性规划

$$\max z = 4x_1 + 3x_2\,,\\
s.t.=\left\{
\begin{aligned}
2x_1 + x_2 & \le & 10\,,\\
x_1 + x_2  & \le & 8\,,\\
x_2        & \le & 7\,,\\
x_1,x_2    & \ge & 0\,.
\end{aligned}
\right.
\,.$$
